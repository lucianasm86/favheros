My Favorite Super Heroes

Wonder Woman

Wonder Woman is a fictional superhero in American comic books. The character is a founding member of the Justice League, goddess, and Ambassador-at-Large of the Amazon people. Some of her abilities are: Superhuman strength, speed, durability, reflexes, and longevity.

Lisa Simpson

Lisa Simpson is the middle child and most intelligent of the Simpson family. Lisa's high intellect and liberal political stance creates a barrier between her and other children her age, therefore she is a bit of a loner and social outcast. Lisa enjoys many hobbies such as reading and playing the saxophone.

Daenerys Targaryen

Princess Daenerys Targaryen, also known as Daenerys Stormborn, is one of the last confirmed members of House Targaryen, along with her older brother Viserys, who refers to her as Dany. She is the youngest child of King Aerys II Targaryen and his sister-wife, Queen Rhaella. Daenerys is alson known as the Mother of Dragons.
